Welcome!

I am a Visiting Assistant Professor and Postdoctoral Researcher at Purdue University, CS department. Previously, I earned my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET), Dhaka, and completed my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University advised by Prof. Kaushik Roy . My research spans efficient machine learning, spiking neural networks, computer vision, and biomedical AI. I have previously served as a lecturer at BUET, BRAC University and Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), and have taught at Purdue. I enjoy teaching, mentoring, and building collaborative learning environments — feel free to connect!
